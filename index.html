About Our Project

Our project aims to provide a platform where willing students can find study partners, share course materials and thus help each other. Thanks to StudyB, students will be able to get together with the people who have taken the course by registering to the pages of the courses they have taken after confirming that they are Bilkent students through bilkent emails. On the course pages, students will be able to share course materials such as course notes and questions and help each other. Students who are looking for a study partner will be able to create study rooms and will be able to work together with these groups either face-to-face or in a virtual environment.
